The Best Movies According To Milanetto

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Chilling Adventures of Sabrina

A dark coming-of-age story that tackles issues like identity, feminism, and family, all while delivering plenty of supernatural thrills.

The Rookie

A gripping sports drama that follows the true story of a 45-year-old rookie pitcher trying to make it in Major League Baseball.

Beauty in Black

A powerful exploration of race, identity, and the complexities of the human experience, all told through the lens of a gripping narrative.
